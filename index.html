Our Story

Where Andhra
Tradition Meets
Modern Grilling

"Every flame we light, every spice we grind — it's our love letter to Andhra Pradesh."

Nestled in the heart of Cuddalore, Beach Grill's Andhra Annam brings you the authentic fire and flavour of Andhra Pradesh. From the crackling of our BBQ grill to the aroma of slow-cooked biryanis, every dish is crafted with passion, fresh ingredients, and traditional recipes. Whether you're craving a fiery Andhra meal, a crispy lollipop, or a refreshing mojito, we have something that will make you come back — again and again.
